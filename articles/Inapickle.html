Silverstone • 4/07/2026

"In a pickle" - Lando Norris

Round 9 of the 2026 Formula 1 season was a difficult weekend for McLaren. A sixth- and eighth-place finish was far from the result the team had hoped for, despite bringing upgrades to the MCL40. The former World Champion Lando Norris is surprised by the pace between their car and the leading cars.

"We're just slow. I thought we improved the car in some areas, but we need to understand why we're just slow in the straight, we're slow in every corner. It's just the car's not very efficient."

Clearly the car has been challenging to work with, his teammate Oscar Piastri agreed with Lando. It's hard to believe that McLaren could win the Grand Prix tomorrow, especially when Silverstone requires a car which performs great on straights. Lando Norris still hopes for a P5, beating the Red Bulls, although he spoke about the car losing time and pace during the race.

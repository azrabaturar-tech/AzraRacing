Max Verstappen's race engineer will be leaving Red Bull for McLaren in 2028

Gianpiero Lambiase will leave Red Bull Racing in 2028 and take a new spot in McLaren as Chief Racing Officer. Lambiase has worked alongside Verstappen since 2016 and both experienced a lot throughout the years. They won four Drivers' Championships together which makes it emotional to see him leave Red Bull. The McLaren F1 Team look forward to welcoming Gianpiero Lambiase when his contract ends, no later than 2028.
